About Us

Connect. Grow. Thrive.

WISE Professionals YPN is Windsor's young professionals network, built by and for the next generation of community leaders in Northern Colorado.

We create space for meaningful connections, professional development, and genuine community. Whether you're new to the area or a lifelong local, WISE is where you'll find your people.

As a committee of the Windsor Chamber of Commerce, we bridge the gap between emerging professionals and the established business community.
